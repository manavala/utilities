Vmanager Steps:
1. Launch the Vmanager
	Cmd: bsub -Ip vmanager &
	It opens the "Incisive Enterprise Manager"
2. Click "Setup" icon
	It adds the default local session
3. Click "Vplan" icon in the same window
	It opens new window "verification Plan tree" & Switch to that window
4. Click "New ep" icon in the same window
	It opens the new window "Enterprise Planner" & Switch to that window
5. In Right side pane, select tab "Specs" next "Plan" tab
	Click "Add a spec"
