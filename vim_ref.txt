#ranges
:21 (@21th line)
:1 (@1st line)
:$ (only last line)
:. (current line)
:% (all lines)
:21,$ (from 21 to end of file)
:.,$ (from current line to end of file)
:.+1,$ (line after current to end of file)
:.,.5 or :.,.+5 (both are same, current line to plus 5 lines)
